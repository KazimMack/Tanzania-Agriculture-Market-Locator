About AgriMarket Tanzania

AgriMarket Tanzania is a platform that connects Tanzanian farmers with local, regional, and international markets. Our mission is to empower smallholder farmers by providing access to reliable marketplaces where they can sell their products for the best prices.

Tanzania has a diverse agricultural economy, from coffee plantations in the Kilimanjaro region to rice farms in Morogoro. We aim to make it easier for farmers to locate buyers, whether in local community markets, large wholesale centers, or export hubs.

Most Popular Products

Here are some of the most sought-after agricultural products in Tanzania that can fetch great prices in local and international markets.

Maize

Maize is one of the top staple foods in Tanzania and is widely grown in regions like Dodoma and Mbeya. It is in high demand in both local markets and for export to neighboring countries.

Coffee

Coffee from the Kilimanjaro region is one of Tanzania's leading export products. Its quality is recognized worldwide, and it’s in high demand in European and American markets.

Cashew Nuts

The southern regions of Mtwara and Lindi are renowned for producing high-quality cashew nuts. These nuts are popular for both local consumption and international export.

Rice

Grown predominantly in Morogoro, Tanzanian rice is a popular product in regional wholesale markets and is also gaining traction for export, particularly within East Africa.

Tea

Tea grown in the Southern Highlands, especially in areas like Njombe and Iringa, is another top export product. It is popular in international markets such as the UK and Middle East.

Cloves

Zanzibar is famous for its production of cloves, which are highly sought after for spices and medicinal purposes. This product is a major export to Asian and European markets.
